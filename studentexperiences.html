Ryan Jennings

At Oklahoma Christian, I have consistently been challenged in my academics. There is always something new to learn. The vast amount of content available can be overwhelming, but the teachers are always readily available to help you. On top of that, there are so many online resources that can help with classes, it is very realistic to consistently succeed in the Computer Science program. Upperclassmen are extremely kind and helpful, and are always willing to give advice on how to work smarter and be successful.

Caleb Bozarth
